Single Origins

Where the Journey Begins

Every origin tells a story shaped by soil, altitude, and the hands that tend it.

🇧🇷

Brazil

Nutty · Chocolate · Low Acidity

The world's largest coffee producer. Brazil's vast plateaus yield beans with a naturally sweet, chocolatey character — the backbone of every great espresso blend.

Altitude: 800–1,300m
🇪🇹

Ethiopia

Floral · Berry · Wine-like

The birthplace of coffee. Ethiopian beans carry a wild, ancestral complexity — where jasmine meets blueberry and the land itself is in the cup.

Altitude: 1,500–2,200m
🇨🇴

Colombia

Caramel · Citrus · Balanced

Grown in the misty Andean highlands, Colombian coffee is the gold standard — bright, clean, and elegantly balanced with a sweetness that lingers.

Altitude: 1,200–2,000m

Brewing Methods

Your Coffee, Your Way

Whole Bean

For those who demand peak freshness. Grind just before brewing to unlock the full spectrum of aroma and flavour locked within each bean.

Ground

Pre-ground to the perfect consistency for filter, French press, or moka. Ready when you are, with no compromise on quality.

Capsules

Compatible with Nespresso® machines. The full depth of a KAFÉ blend in a single, perfectly measured capsule — luxurious simplicity.

Instant

Freeze-dried at the peak of perfection. No machine required — just the ritual of a thoughtful cup wherever life takes you.
